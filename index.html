Our Mission

Deforestation, climate change and water pollution are having a profound impact on our wildlife, and here at the Ontario Bird Sanctuary, we seek to protect endangered species of birds from extinction. With your help, we can also create a database of birds in the wild for future generations to see.

Birds play an important role in the very delicate ecosystem of wildlife. Undoubtedly, thier most important role is to pollinate flowers and disper, se seeds. This provides food to other animals and wildlife, and without their help, many species of plants will go extinct which can significantly impact a local ecosystem. They are also a natural pest control, eating various agricultural pests.
